Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 2 cup all purpose flour
  2. 100 ml tomato ketchup
  3. 1 tomato
  4. 2 onion
  5. 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  6. 1 teaspoon baking powder
  7. 1 teaspoon sugar
  8. 2 teaspoon virgin olive oil
  9. 100 gm processed cheese
  10. 4 mushroom
  11. 1/2 capsicum (green pepper)
  12. 1 teaspoon oregano
  13. 1/2 cup mozzarella
  14. 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  15. water as required

Steps for making pizza

  • Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to the temperature specified in your pizza dough recipe or on the packaging.
  • Prepare Pizza Dough: If you're making your own dough, follow a recipe or use a pre-made dough. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  • Prepare Toppings: Chop or slice your chosen toppings into bite-sized pieces. If using meat, make sure it's cooked before putting it on the pizza.
  • Assemble Pizza: Place the rolled-out dough onto a pizza stone or baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust. Sprinkle cheese evenly over the sauce, then add your desired toppings.
  • Bake: Carefully transfer the assembled pizza into the preheated oven. Bake according to the dough recipe or package instructions, usually around 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Cool and Serve: Once baked, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. This allows the cheese to set slightly and makes it easier to cut. Serve hot and enjoy!

Note

Remember, you can customize your pizza with different sauces, cheeses, and toppings to suit your taste preferences. Experiment with different combinations to find your favorite!
